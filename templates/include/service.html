Internet Services

Afghanistan Research and Education Network (AfgREN) is Afghanistan's national research and education network (REN). Which provides internet access for universities.

Research

AfgREN is a project from Higher Education ministry of Afghanistan that aims to implement, manage and extend sustainable e-Infrastructures dedicated for the research and education communities and to boost scientific research and cooperation in member Universities through the provision of world-class e-Infrastructures and e-services.
